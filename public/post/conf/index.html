Second Behavioral Macroeconomics Workshop

Heterogeneity and Expectations in Macroeconomics and Finance

Jan 16, 2019 1 min read

With great pleasure, I want to draw your attention to the call for papers of the Second Behavioral Macroeocnomcis Workshop, that we are organizing in the city of Bamberg on June 14 and 15.

I really enjoyed the high quality presentations and interesting conversations of last year’s edition, and I am confident that this year’s workshop will be even better. The theme of the workshop will be ‘Heterogeneity and Expectations in Macroeconomics and Finance’. As indicated in the call for papers, however, we encourage submissions from a broader spectrum of behavioral macroeconomics and finance topics.

Confirmed Keynote speakers are George W. Evans and Domenico Delli Gatti. More information can be found on the conference website.
